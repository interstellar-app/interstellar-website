## Features

<CardGrid stagger>
  <Card title="Multi-client support">
    Available for **Android**, **Linux**, and **Windows** devices, with more to
    come. [Install now!](/install/)
  </Card>
  <Card title="Multi-server support">
    Connects to multiple Fediverse software, including
    [**Mbin**](https://joinmbin.org), [**Lemmy**](https://join-lemmy.org), and
    [**PieFed**](https://join.piefed.social).
  </Card>
  <Card title="Numerous accounts with guests">
    Sign in to as many Fediverse accounts as you wish, or browse anonymously as
    a guest!
  </Card>
  <Card title="Settings Profiles">
    Save, switch, and even [share](https://kbin.earth/m/interstellar_configs)
    between groups of settings with ease. [Learn more here.](/features/profiles)
  </Card>
  <Card title="Filter Lists">
    Filter out unwanted content from your feed with highly customizable (and
    [sharable](https://kbin.earth/m/interstellar_configs)) keyword filters. You
    can even use **regular expressions** if you so please.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Markdown Editor with Drafts">
    Comes with a **detailed toolbar**, **keyboard shortcuts**, and a **Preview
    tab**. Also comes with automatic and manual **draft support**, so you don't
    lose your those precious comments you were about to post.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Free and Open Source">
    Interstellar is 100% free to use and is also [open
    source](https://github.com/interstellar-app/interstellar)! Please consider
    [sponsoring us](https://github.com/sponsors/jwr1) or even
    [contributing](/contributing) üôè
  </Card>
</CardGrid>

For even more features, be sure to checkout the Features section in the [documentation](/docs)!
